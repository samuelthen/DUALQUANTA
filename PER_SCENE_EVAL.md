# Per-Scene Evaluation Results

Per-scene PSNR/SSIM/LPIPS for every checkpoint reported in the paper, computed from raw per-window
eval logs in `optical_flow_ablation`, `spad-net`, and `spad-net-color` (this project's earlier working
directories). Every model below was matched to its DUALQUANTA checkpoint by exact `(epoch,
best_psnr_gam)` metadata, not filename similarity alone — e.g. `single_frame_ng` was ruled out despite
an almost-identical score to `single_frame_nafnet.pth`, and `color_a3_i2k` (no suffix) was identified as
`DUALQUANTA_T11_stage2` this way.

31 i2-2kfps test scenes throughout. All Table 1 PSNR/SSIM cells are the mean over that scene's sliding
windows; Table 3/4 cells come from each run's own per-window log, aggregated the same way.
